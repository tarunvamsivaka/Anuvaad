Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Anuvaad ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered code translation service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services at anuvaad.dev.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (provided during sign-up)
  • Authentication data via Supabase (hashed passwords, OAuth tokens)

Usage Data

  • Code snippets submitted for translation (processed in real-time, not stored permanently)
  • Translation history (stored server-side in your account)
  • Feature usage metrics (translation count, modes used)

Payment Information

  • Stripe handles all payment processing. We never see or store your full credit card number.
  • We store your subscription status (plan type, active/cancelled) in our database.

Technical Data

  • IP address (for rate limiting, not stored long-term)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service: Translate code, generate code, and manage your account.
  • Process payments: Manage Pro subscriptions via Stripe.
  • Rate limiting: Prevent abuse and ensure fair usage for all users.
  • Improve the service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features.
  • Communication: Send account-related emails (e.g., payment receipts, subscription changes).

4. Code Data Processing

Your code is not stored on our servers. Code submitted for translation is sent to Groq and DeepSeek AI for processing. The translated response is returned to your browser immediately. We do not maintain a database of submitted code.

Translation history is stored securely in your account database.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Groq (Llama 3.3 70B) and DeepSeek (V3/R1) — Processes code translations.
  • Supabase — Authentication and database. Subject to Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe — Payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active.
  • Subscription records are retained for billing compliance.
  • Rate limiting data is stored in Redis with configurable retention.
  • You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) for database access control
  • Stripe PCI-DSS compliant payment handling
  • Environment variables for all secret keys (never hardcoded)

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your translation history (available via the Export feature)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

9. Cookies

We use minimal cookies/storage:

  • Authentication tokens (Supabase session) — required for sign-in
  • Theme preference (localStorage) — dark/light mode choice
  • Translation history (localStorage) — your saved translations
  • Usage counter (server-side) — daily translation count

We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Anuvaad is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
