How Anuvaad Works

01
Step One

Paste your code.

Drop in any snippet — a function, a class, an entire module. Python, Java, C++, Go, SQL. Anuvaad reads everything, even minified or obfuscated code.

02
Step Two

Gemini understands it.

Anuvaad sends your code to Gemini 2.5 Flash, which parses intent, context, and structure — not just syntax. The result is deep understanding, not shallow substitution.

03
Step Three

Read, edit, export.

You get a structured explanation, syntax-highlighted and block-by-block. Edit any part to refactor the original code. Export as Markdown, JSON, or PDF for your team.
