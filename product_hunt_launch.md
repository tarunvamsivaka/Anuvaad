## 3. Maker Comment (First Comment)
*Post this immediately after the product goes live. It sets the tone, explains the "why," and encourages discussion.*

**Hey Product Hunt! 👋 I’m the creator of Anuvaad.**

If you’ve ever been dropped into a completely undocumented, decades-old legacy codebase, or had to rapidly port logic from a language you barely know—you know exactly how frustrating code archaeology can be. 

I built Anuvaad because I was tired of bouncing between standard chat interfaces that lose formatting, strip out comments, and fail to adhere to strict corporate coding standards. 

**What is Anuvaad?**
Anuvaad is an AI-powered code translation workspace specifically engineered for software teams. It operates in three main modes:
1. **Code → English:** Paste obscure, highly-complex code and receive a block-by-block breakdown of exactly what the logic is doing.
2. **English → Code:** Describe your requirements in plain English, and Anuvaad generates syntactically perfect, production-ready code.
3. **Code → Code:** Instantly convert logic between 35+ different programming languages (e.g., porting an old Java backend into a modern Go service).

**Under the Hood ⚡**
Speed and accuracy are everything. The core engine is built on **FastAPI** and uses a dynamic LLM routing system. We use **Groq (Llama 3.3)** for extreme, near-instant streaming speed on simpler tasks, and dynamically route to **DeepSeek V3** for deep-reasoning on complex, undocumented legacy systems.

**Why not just use Copilot or ChatGPT?**
Copilot is phenomenal for inline autocomplete within your IDE, but it isn’t built for macro-level architectural translation or deep codebase explanation. ChatGPT is highly conversational and prone to formatting errors. Anuvaad is a dedicated, distraction-free environment built strictly for code conversion, featuring Team Workspaces, translation history, and the ability to inject your company's custom coding standards into every generation.

🎁 **Launch Offer:** We have a generous free tier available today. You can try the core translation engine right now without a credit card. 

I'd love to hear your feedback, feature requests, or thoughts on the UI! I’ll be hanging out in the comments all day to answer your questions.

## 6. Ideal Day and Time to Launch
**Target:** Tuesday or Wednesday at 12:01 AM PT (8:01 AM UTC).
**Reasoning:** The Product Hunt homepage resets every day at exactly 12:01 AM Pacific Time. Launching at this exact minute gives you the maximum 24 hours of exposure to accumulate votes before the cycle resets. Tuesdays and Wednesdays are historically the highest-traffic days on the platform, giving you the best chance of visibility from tech journalists and early adopters.
